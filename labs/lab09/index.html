My Favorite Recipe





The best chicken alfredo created!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Spinach (fresh)
  • Linguine noodles
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian cheese blend
  • Butter
  • Adobo seasoning
  • Paprika
  • Creole seasoning
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Fresh parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Minced garlic



Cooking Instructions

  1. Clean chicken breast
  2. Use all of your seasonings to coat both sides of chicken breast
  3. Boil linguine noodles until cooked to your liking in water with a pinch of salt and a tbl spoon of olive oil
  4. Drain noodles once done and sit to the side
  5. Put a spoon full of butter in a seperate pan with olive oil
  6. Cook chicken breast in pan until fully cooked through and no pink
  7. Set cooked chicken to the side
  8. In same pan you cooked the chicken breast, add more butter and your minced garlic
  9. Cook the garlic down some and then add your cherry tomatoes and spinach
  10. Add your heavy whipping cream to the same pan
  11. Stir until the heavy whipping cream starts to bubble a little
  12. Turn heat down on stove
  13. Start adding your cheese in slowly while stirring at the same time until your desired thickness
  14. Once to your liking, add your cooked noodles in
  15. Stir everything together while stovetop is on low
  16. Place cooked chicken on top and serve
