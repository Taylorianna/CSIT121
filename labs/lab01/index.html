My Favorite Recipe

The best chicken alfredo created!

  • Chicken breast
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Spinach (fresh)
  • Linguine noodles
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian cheese blend
  • Butter
  • Adobo seasoning
  • Paprika
  • Creole seasoning
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Fresh parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Minced garlic

Cooking Instructions

  • Clean chicken breast
  • Use all of your seasonings to coat both sides of chicken breast
  • Boil linguine noodles until cooked to your liking in water with a pinch of salt and a tbl spoon of olive oil
  • Drain noodles once done and sit to the side
  • Put a spoon full of butter in a seperate pan with olive oil
  • Cook chicken breast in pan until fully cooked through and no pink
  • Set cooked chicken to the side
  • In same pan you cooked the chicken breast, add more butter and your minced garlic
  • Cook the garlic down some and then add your cherry tomatoes and spinach
  • Add your heavy whipping cream to the same pan
  • Stir until the heavy whipping cream starts to bubble a little
  • Turn heat down on stove
  • Start adding your cheese in slowly while stirring at the same time until your desired thickness
  • Once to your liking, add your cooked noodles in
  • Stir everything together while stovetop is on low
  • Place cooked chicken on top and serve
